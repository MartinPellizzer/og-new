The data used in this meta-analysis were collected from peer-reviewed papers published between 1990 and 2024. A total of 31 studies were included, which reported the effects of elevated O3 on MDA content, SOD, and POD activities in rice, wheat, and ornamental herbs. The studies were selected based on specific criteria, including plant species, exposure duration, and O3 concentration. The data were extracted from tables or figures using graph digitizing software, and a total of 343 sets of observations were included in the meta-analysis.
