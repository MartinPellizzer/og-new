Ozone therapy has gained significant attention in the dental community due to its antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and tissue-regenerative properties. This review aims to provide a comprehensive overview of ozone treatment in various fields of dentistry, including oral pathology, conservative dentistry, endodontics, periodontics, pedodontics, oral surgery, oral medicine, and temporomandibular joint (TMJ) problems. The goal is to establish the long-term advantages of ozone therapy in dentistry and standardize treatment regimens.
