It also analyzed time series data to assess the impact of emission reduction policies on O3 concentration improvements in China, revealing changes in O3 levels over different regions and periods. Overall, the study provides valuable insights into the effects of pollution reduction measures on atmospheric composition and air quality in China, with implications for policy-making and environmental management.
