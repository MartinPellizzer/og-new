TITLE:
Efficacy of microalgae removal by ozone pilot water treatment in a subtropical South American reservoir

AUTHOR:
Salusso, María M.* ; Liliana B. Moraña

ISTITUTIONS:
Facultad de Ciencias Naturales, Universidad Nacional Salta. Avenida Bolivia 5150, (4400) Salta, Argentina

DATE:
Published online: 16 May 2023

TAGS:
Phytoplankton, water, microalgae 


---------------------------------------------------

problem:
	Phytoplankton removal by ozone pretreatment

experiment:
	Cryptophyceae, Chlorophyceae, Dinophyceae and Trebouxiophyceae in spring and Cyanobacteria in summer

result:
	reduction in algal density (mean 26.94%)
	Zygnematophyceae (68.21%), Euglenophyceae (59.80%), Cyanobacteria (57.87%), Cryptophyceae (53.84%),
	Dinophyceae (38.59%) and Chlorophyceae (28.66%)
		
objective:
	ozone removed >50%.
