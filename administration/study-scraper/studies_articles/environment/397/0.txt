Ozone (O3) pollution is a significant environmental concern globally, posing risks to human health and ecosystems. The study published in Environmental Monitoring and Assessment investigated the characteristics of surface ozone pollution and its correlations with meteorological factors in Xiangtan, China. The researchers analyzed ozone monitoring data for Xiangtan and meteorological observation data from 2020 to 2022. This study aimed to explore the effects of local emissions and meteorological factors on daily maximum 8-h average ozone concentrations.
