Here is a summary of the study in 1 detailed paragraph, focusing on the information and data related to ozone:

A study conducted by the Organismo de Evaluación y Fiscalización Ambiental (OEFA) monitored air quality in the Alberto Fujimori settlement in Huacho, Peru, during an episode of solid waste burning from February 11-16, 2015. The study found that ozone (O3) concentrations registered during the monitoring period did not exceed the national standards for ambient air quality. Additionally, the highest concentrations of gases and particulate matter were recorded on February 12, when a maximum intensity of smoke was observed emanating from the municipal landfill within the monitoring period.
