MethodsMeteorological data were obtained from NASA Power website. Ozone air quality data were measured using Backman model 950A ozone Analyzer, which detects concentrations as low as 0.05 ppm, with measurements taken every 40 seconds. From January to October 2023, ozone concentrations increased during the dry months of May to October, with the highest hourly value recorded at 263 μg/m³.
