Here is a summarized version of the study in one paragraph, focusing on information and data related to ozone:

This clinical study evaluated the effects of anti-aging integrative therapy, which included ozone therapy, on telomere length and biological aging symptoms. The treatment was given to 36 individuals, and results showed that 97.22% of participants experienced an increase in telomere length, with an average increase of 0.43 Kbp. Additionally, 94.44% of participants had a reduced biological age, with an average decrease of 3.8 years, indicating a statistically significant improvement. The study also found that the treatment showed improvements in various aging symptoms, with no major adverse events reported.
