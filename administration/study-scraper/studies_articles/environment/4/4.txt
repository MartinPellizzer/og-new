In conclusion, this study demonstrates the potential of Cu2S/Ni3S2@NF catalyst for efficient removal of PNP from wastewater through catalytic ozonation. The results provide new insights into the mechanism and pathways of PNP degradation and highlight the importance of optimizing reaction conditions to achieve high removal rates. This study contributes to the development of sustainable technologies for wastewater treatment and provides a feasible pathway for the removal of phenolic pollutants.
