The effectiveness of medical ozone therapy in preventing compromised nasal skin in revision rhinoplasty has been a topic of interest among plastic surgeons. Revision rhinoplasty is a complex surgical procedure that requires meticulous attention to detail to achieve optimal results. The use of ozone therapy as an adjunctive treatment has shown promising results in enhancing wound healing and reducing the risk of complications. This study aimed to investigate the potential benefits of medical ozone therapy in preventing compromised nasal skin in revision rhinoplasty patients.
