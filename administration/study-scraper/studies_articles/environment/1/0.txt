The study explores the weekly cycle of ozone concentrations in Arizona, examining differences between weekdays and weekends to understand the impact of anthropogenic activities on ozone fluctuations. The research aims to advance insights into ozone concentration temporal cycles, focusing on six sites across Arizona and a more detailed look at the Phoenix metropolitan area. By analyzing data from January 2015 to December 2021, this study compares O3 and NO2 levels based on day of the week and time of day.
