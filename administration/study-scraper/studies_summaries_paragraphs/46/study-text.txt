The study investigated the oxidation mechanism of the earth's atmosphere, involving the reaction of chemical species with oxygen-containing compounds. The Chapman cycle, which includes catalytic cycles and ozone loss/regeneration, was modeled using theoretical models and software such as CHEMKIN Pro, Gaussian 16, and ChemRate. Ozone (O3) plays a crucial role in this mechanism, and its rate constants were determined experimentally; however, these values include uncertainties due to experimental processes.
