Abstract
The final work focuses on the study and design of a lunar orbit satellite mission for observation and navigation. The project is divided into theoretical and practical blocks. The theoretical block covers essential aspects of satellites in lunar orbit, while the practical block focuses on the simulation of satellites in orbit, using advanced technology for navigation and observation. The study includes a detailed analysis of the lunar environment, comparing it with the Earth and describing its terrain, temperatures and heat flow.
