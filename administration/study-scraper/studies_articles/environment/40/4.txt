In conclusion, this study provides valuable insights into the spatiotemporal characteristics of O3 pollution in Siping, Northeast China. The findings suggest that O3 pollution is a complex issue requiring joint control measures from various sectors. Additionally, the study highlights the importance of considering regional transport and biogenic sources in O3 pollution prevention strategies. Future research should focus on refining the source apportionment analysis and exploring effective mitigation measures for O3 pollution in Siping.
