Chronic pain in the lower limbs is a prevalent condition that affects millions of people worldwide. It can be caused by various factors such as traumatic injuries, degenerative joint diseases, peripheral neuropathies, and circulatory disorders. Conventional treatments for chronic pain often have limited efficacy and potential adverse side effects, leading to a search for alternative therapies. Ozonotherapy, also known as ozone therapy, is a treatment that involves the use of ozone (O3) to promote healing and reduce pain. This systematic review aims to evaluate the effectiveness of ozonotherapy in treating lower limb pain.
