Here is a 60-80 word summary focusing on the information and data related to ozone:

A systematic review and meta-analysis of 11 studies found that ozone therapy significantly reduced foot ulcer size (Standardized Mean Difference: -25.84, p = 0.05), shortened mean healing time (SMD: -38.59, p < 0.001), decreased hospital length of stay (SMD: -8.75, p < 0.001), and lowered amputation rates (Relative Risk: 0.46, p < 0.001) in patients with diabetes-related foot ulcers compared to standard treatment.
