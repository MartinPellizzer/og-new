The authors conducted a systematic search in the ScienceDirect, PubMed, and SciELO databases using a structured combination of specific keywords. Literature review studies and clinical trials relevant to the theme were included. The inclusion criteria involved the selection of studies in English, Portuguese, and Spanish that addressed the application of ozonotherapy in the treatment of pain in the lower limbs. We excluded articles that had no direct relationship with the theme, as well as case reports, case series, coursework, theses, and dissertations.
